WHAT IS PADCATCH?

Created by high school students, PadCatch is a game that draws attention to underprivileged females who can't afford or don't have access to menstural products. Based on the game's user activity, our sponsors will help us donate pads and tampons to women in homeless shelters and prisons. Because menstrual products shouldn't be a "luxury". Period.

WHY IS THIS SO IMPORTANT?

The period is a natural process that women go through each month, and we believe that having access to menstrual products is a necessity. Underprivileged women shouldn't have to feel uncomfortable each month because of a natural bodily function.
